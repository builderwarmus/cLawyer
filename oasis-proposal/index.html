Flagged-Entry Protection

Premium · from $9,500

Facial-recognition screening of new sign-ups against a retained banned-member list, alerting management on a match.

  • ◆A high-value security feature for a members' venue with a genuine banned-guest problem
  • ◆Requires privacy/legal review first — biometric data under PIPEDA needs explicit consent, a retention/deletion policy and counsel sign-off
  • ◆We'd build only after that's in place
