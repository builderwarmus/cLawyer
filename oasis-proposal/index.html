Every feature in this proposal exists for one reason: to move a stranger, one step at a time, into a loyal member who spends more and comes back. Nothing here is decoration — it's a system. Hover a step to trace the path.

01 The Website

Descend through the four floors

A bespoke, image-led site built around the club's signature idea: scrolling through the four floors as if descending through the building — from the lounge, into the waters, through the playrooms, down to the private quarters. Elegant, dark, gold-accented and fully responsive — a world away from the current template. (You're inside a working preview of it right now.)

Custom Website Build

Phase 1 · Now

Design and front-end build of the full experience, ready for your photography.

  • ◆Bespoke UI/UX in the Oasis brand — dark, gold & aqua, boutique typography
  • ◆Signature "descend through the four floors" scroll experience with a vintage elevator panel, parallax & live floor status
  • ◆19+ age gate; interactive events calendar — click a night to open its flyer, save it, set a reminder or invite a guest
  • ◆Boutique About with Meet the Owners, cinematic media gallery, and a video section that upsells merch, member benefits & partner sponsors
  • ◆Standalone House Rules — general plus tailored guidelines for Couples, Women & Trans Folks, Solo Males and Trans & LGBTQ+ guests, site-wide
  • ◆Accurate day-membership & admission pages (pay-per-visit, extended memberships) with clear calls-to-action
  • ◆Live chat & lead capture — captures messages, member IDs or emails, and promo-consent into your database
  • ◆Private Hire section — off-hours venue rental (private shows, content creators, film & photo shoots) with enquiry capture
  • ◆Self-serve CMS, mobile-first responsive build, SEO foundations, analytics & launch
02 Brand Photography & Video

Class, not amateur

The design is only as striking as the visuals behind it. The current clips — muddy audio, uneven pacing, a script that tries too hard — work against a premium brand. We recommend a dedicated, discreet on-site shoot: a fully-owned, on-brand image and video library that respects the club's strict no-guest-photography policy.

On-Site Photo & Video Production

Recommended

Captured after-hours by our production team; you approve every frame before it's used.

  • ◆Discreet after-hours session across all four floors — lounge, pool & courtyard, playrooms, private quarters
  • ◆Styled interior, ambience, bar, pool & sauna photography (no guests; fully consented talent only if used)
  • ◆Polished short-form vertical video to replace the current clips — for the hero, social & ads
  • ◆Professional editing, colour grading, clean audio & retouching
  • ◆Full commercial usage rights — an image library Oasis owns outright
We built this concept on Oasis's existing photos on purpose — no fabricated renders, no stock. The design elevates what you already have. Now imagine this exact experience with a directed shoot behind it: consistent lighting, cinematic composition, drone & establishing shots, high-end video loops. Every page you've seen quietly upgrades.
03 The Members App

The differentiator

A private companion app that lets members connect, transact and stay engaged — lifting spend-per-visit and repeat visits. We recommend launching as an installable web app (PWA): works on every phone, no app-store approval, with native apps as funded future add-ons. Modules phase to manage budget.

Core Membership Platform

Phase 1 · Now

The foundation every other module plugs into.

  • ◆Secure, age-verified member accounts & profiles
  • ◆Digital membership card (Apple & Google Wallet) for fast, private door entry
  • ◆"Attending tonight" signal, live anonymized count & incognito mode
  • ◆Double-opt-in QR connections & discreet moderated chat
  • ◆Paginated "who's here tonight" directory

Commerce & Bookings

Phase 1 · Now

Where the app pays for itself — every module below is a revenue stream.

  • ◆Payments & commerce — day membership, extended membership, advance tickets to sell-out nights (Stripe)
  • ◆Merchandise & enhancements — branded merch and add-ons with member pricing
  • ◆Reservations — hourly private-room booking calendar (1 PM–2 AM)
  • ◆Private venue hire — enquiries for off-hours rental (shows, content, film, corporate, parties)
  • ◆In-venue bar ordering — drinks from either bar, routed to staff
  • ◆DJ song requests — member requests queued to the booth

Community, Safety & Admin

Phase 1 · Now

Engagement, member wellbeing, and the back-office to run it all.

  • ◆Community & perks — mailing list, "231 Mutual Connections" forum, SDC.com link, partner-discount network
  • ◆Discreet safety alert — an "I need help" button that quietly notifies staff to a member's location
  • ◆Staff admin console — manage events, menus, reservation slots, members & content

Flagged-Entry Protection

Premium · from $9,500

Facial-recognition screening of new sign-ups against a retained banned-member list, alerting management on a match.

  • ◆A high-value security feature for a members' venue with a genuine banned-guest problem
  • ◆Requires privacy/legal review first — biometric data under PIPEDA needs explicit consent, a retention/deletion policy and counsel sign-off
  • ◆We'd build only after that's in place
